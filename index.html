Welcome recruit to the UMPD home page

The United Miko Police Department is a VRChat role-play community our main goal is to make a VRChat community where everybody can meet & make new friends & feel safe, We started off as a community that didn't really have much to offer for a while but a few weeks later we started getting things done, We are like the trust & safety on VRChat we try to keep VRChat a fun & safe community where everybody, Can have fun & enjoy the VRChat communities worlds what it got to offer, try to enforce the law/rules, Also to just have fun & role-play, we are more like a family than a normal RP group we do our best to make everybody feel welcome & to be themselves.
